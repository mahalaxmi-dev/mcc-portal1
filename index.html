About MCC Grievance Portal

Empowering Citizens Through Digital Governance

Transforming Urban Governance

The Mangaluru City Corporation (MCC) Grievance Portal represents a groundbreaking initiative in civic engagement and digital governance. As the official online platform for citizen grievance redressal, we are committed to fostering transparency, accountability, and efficiency in municipal services delivery.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and administration, ensuring that every voice is heard and every concern addressed with the urgency and attention it deserves. Through this digital platform, we are building a more responsive, inclusive, and citizen-centric Mangaluru.
