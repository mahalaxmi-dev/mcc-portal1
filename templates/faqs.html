Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about filing complaints, tracking status, and using the MCC Grievance Portal.

Who can file a complaint? ▼
Any citizen of Mangalore can register and file a complaint through this portal. You must have a registered account to file complaints.
What kind of complaints can I file? ▼
Only civic issues related to Mangalore are allowed — such as sanitation, roads, water supply, drainage, street lights, and parks. Personal disputes are not allowed.
How do I track my complaint? ▼
You can track your complaint using the Complaint ID provided after submission. Use the Track Complaint page on the home screen — no login required.
How long does it take to resolve a complaint? ▼
Resolution time depends on the nature of the complaint and the department. You can check the status anytime through your dashboard or the track complaint page.
Can I file a complaint without registering? ▼
No. You must register and login to file a complaint. However, tracking a complaint by ID is available to everyone without login.
What happens if I file a false complaint? ▼
False complaints may be rejected by the department officer. Please ensure your complaint is genuine and related to a civic issue in Mangalore.
Can I file complaints via email? ▼
No. Complaints sent through email will not be officially registered. Please use this grievance portal to file complaints.
Can I edit or delete a complaint after filing? ▼
No. Once a complaint is submitted it cannot be edited or deleted. Please review your complaint carefully before submitting.
What is the attachment requirement? ▼
Attaching a photo or document as evidence is mandatory when filing a complaint. This helps the department officer understand and verify the issue faster.
I forgot my username or password. What do I do? ▼
On the login page, click "Forgot Username?" to recover your username using your registered email. Click "Forgot Password?" to reset your password using your username and registered email.
Can I file multiple complaints? ▼
Yes. You can file as many complaints as needed. Each complaint will get a unique Complaint ID and can be tracked separately from your dashboard.
Who handles my complaint after I file it? ▼
Your complaint is automatically routed to the department you selected. The department officer will review it and update the status to In Progress or Resolved.
