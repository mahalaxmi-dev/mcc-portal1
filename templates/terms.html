📋 Before You File a Complaint

Please read and agree to the following rules

  • 📍 Complaint must be from Mangalore city only.
  • 🏙️ Only civic issues are allowed — sanitation, roads, water, drainage, lights, parks.
  • 🚫 Personal disputes and non-civic issues are not accepted.
  • ⚠️ False complaints may be rejected by the department officer.
  • 📎 A photo or document attachment is mandatory when filing a complaint.
  • 🔒 Once submitted, complaints cannot be edited or deleted.
