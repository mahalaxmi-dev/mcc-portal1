About MCC Grievance Portal

The Mangalore City Corporation (MCC) Grievance Portal delivers a fast, transparent, and citizen-first way to report civic issues. It connects residents with municipal departments, tracks progress clearly, and helps improve service delivery across the city.

Trusted by citizens

Every complaint is routed to the appropriate department and managed through a secure municipal workflow.

Action-oriented reporting

Report issues with photos, location details, and department selection to accelerate resolution.

Real-time transparency

Track complaint status, receive updates, and know when your issue is assigned or resolved.

How It Works

4 simple steps from registration to resolution.
1

Register

Create your citizen account using name, email, phone number, and username to start filing complaints.

2

File a complaint

Select the correct department, describe the issue clearly, add supporting photos, and pin the exact location.

3

Track progress

Monitor updates and status changes in real time through the portal using your complaint ID.

4

Get it resolved

Responsible officers review the issue, take action, and update the complaint until it is resolved.

Coverage areas

Supports grievances related to sanitation, roads, water supply, drainage, street lighting, parks, and other civic services.

Citizen-first experience

Designed for simplicity and clarity so every resident can raise concerns without friction.

Transparent workflow

From submission to action, complaints are visible and follow a consistent municipal process.
